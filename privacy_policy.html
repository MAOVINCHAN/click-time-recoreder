隐私政策 Privacy Policy

数据处理原则 Data Processing Principles

我们承诺所有数据处理均在用户设备本地完成，不会上传任何数据到服务器，也不进行任何联网操作。

We commit that all data processing is done locally on the user's device, with no data uploaded to servers or any online operations performed.

权限说明 Permission Explanation

我们遵循最小权限原则，仅请求运行所必需的最低权限。用户可以随时在系统设置中更改权限。

We follow the principle of least privilege, requesting only the minimum permissions necessary for operation. Users can modify permissions at any time through system settings.

不依赖第三方 Independent from Third Parties

本产品不依赖任何第三方服务或外部依赖，确保数据处理的完全自主控制。

This product does not rely on any third-party services or external dependencies, ensuring complete control over data processing.

数据安全措施 Data Security Measures

所有数据仅保存在浏览器本地存储中。当用户卸载本产品时，所有相关数据将自动删除。

All data is stored only in the browser's local storage. All related data will be automatically deleted when the product is uninstalled.
